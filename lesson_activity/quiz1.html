Simple Sponge Cake

    Ingredents
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 85gself-raising flour


  1. Notes on Ingredents
    2.
  2. caster sugar
    • Finely granulated white sugar
  3. Self raising flour
    • A pre-mixed combination of flour and leavening agents(usually salt and baking powder).


    1. Method
    • Preheat the oven to 190cc.
    • Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
    • In a medium bowl, whip together the eggs and caster sugar until flutty.
    • Fold in four
    • Pour mixture into the prepared pan.
    • Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven until the top of the cake springs back when lightly pressed.
    • Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
